Cehami extract (derived from *Centipeda cunninghamii*, a flowering plant native to Australia) is in the Revive+ Face Scrub because it calms irritation and supports skin comfort during physical exfoliation. Revive+ Face Scrub extract comes from a plant long used in traditional Australian medicine for its skin-soothing properties.

Physical exfoliation temporarily disrupts the skin's surface, and cehami extract addresses that response directly. The botanical compounds in *Centipeda cunninghamii* reduce redness and discomfort at the site of application, making the scrubbing action gentler on skin that is sensitive, reactive, or prone to post-exfoliation tightness. Research involving 65 human subjects assessed over 16 weeks demonstrates that botanical extracts produce measurable improvements in skin parameters when applied consistently (Tarshish et al., Journal of Cosmetic Dermatology, 2022).

The Era Organics Revive+ pairs cehami extract with aloe vera and oat amino protein so the Gentle Face Exfoliant calms as it exfoliates rather than leaving the skin feeling raw. The result is a formula designed for repeated use, two to three times per week, without the cumulative irritation that physical scrubs sometimes cause. Patch test the Manuka Honey Face Scrub on the inner arm before the first use, particularly for skin that reacts to botanical extracts.
