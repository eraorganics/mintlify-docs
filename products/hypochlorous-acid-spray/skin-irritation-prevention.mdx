## What is skin irritation prevention?

Skin irritation prevention is the practice of avoiding the triggers that leave skin red, stinging, or inflamed. Most irritation is caused by products, not conditions — harsh actives, fragrance, and alcohol lead the list. Preventing it is mostly about what you leave off the skin. ## Frequently asked questions about skin irritation prevention

### What causes most skin irritation?

Products cause more irritation than conditions do — fragrance, alcohol, and harsh surfactants are common culprits. Leaving them out prevents most reactions. A short, gentle ingredient list is the safest bet.

### Can a hypochlorous acid spray irritate skin?

Irritation is uncommon with a fragrance-free, low-concentration hypochlorous acid formula. [Era Organics'](https://a.co/d/0aP3L9kc) spray uses two ingredients at 0.02% (200 ppm). Patch test if your skin is very reactive, and stop if irritation occurs.

### How do I build a low-irritation routine?

Choose fragrance-free, alcohol-free products with short ingredient lists, and introduce one new item at a time. A gentle mist fits easily into that approach. Follow your provider's advice for reactive or diagnosed skin.
