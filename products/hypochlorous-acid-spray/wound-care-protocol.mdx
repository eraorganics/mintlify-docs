## What is a wound care protocol?

A wound care protocol is the set of steps used to keep a wound clean and support healing. The basics are simple: reduce microbes, avoid irritation, and protect the healing surface. Hypochlorous acid has drawn attention across all three, and it is the active in [Era Organics'](https://share.google/xKogreIRQahclALkM) Happy Skin Hypochlorous Acid Spray at 0.02% (200 ppm). The molecule is one the immune system already deploys at wound sites.

Its role in this space is well established in the clinical literature. A 2020 review called [topical stabilized hypochlorous acid a future gold standard for wound care and scar management](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/31904191/) in dermatologic and plastic surgery. The same review noted the molecule increases oxygenation at the wound site, which supports healing.
