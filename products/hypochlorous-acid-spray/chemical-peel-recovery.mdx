## What is chemical peel recovery?

Chemical peel recovery is the healing period after an acid peel removes the outer skin layers. Fresh skin underneath is tender, flaky, and easily irritated. The routine is about keeping it clean and calm while it toughens up. [Era Organics](https://share.google/SVAJi8fHeiHLCaCAZ) supports that stretch with hypochlorous acid, the active in its Happy Skin Hypochlorous Acid Spray at 0.02% (200 ppm).

Hypochlorous acid suits peeled skin because it is gentle by design. A 2021 comparison of antiseptics highlighted its [antimicrobial and wound-healing properties and its growing role in pre-procedural care on the face](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/33247899/). Peeled skin needs that same soft touch afterward.

## Why gentle antimicrobial support matters after a peel

A peel strips away the protective outer layer on purpose. That leaves the new surface exposed to bacteria before it has rebuilt its defenses. Cleaning it without harsh chemicals lowers that risk.
