## What is a spray delivery system?

A spray delivery system is the nozzle and bottle that turn a liquid into a fine, even spray. For skincare, the format decides how the product touches the skin. [Era Organics](https://share.google/7g0Gu4tgcq7aelXbI) chose a spray for Happy Skin Hypochlorous Acid Spray because the delivery method matters as much as the active inside it.

The reason is contact. A spray covers skin without a hand, a cotton pad, or any rubbing. For sensitive or freshly treated skin, that no-touch application is the whole point.

## Why a spray suits hypochlorous acid

Hypochlorous acid works on contact across a surface. A spray lays it down evenly, reaching the whole area at once. A cream or wipe would concentrate it in spots and add friction.

The format has clinical backing for delicate skin. A 2025 study used a [hypochlorous acid mist to support healing after facial laser treatment](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40832716/), where touching the skin is not an option. The related method is covered in [misting technology](/products/hypochlorous-acid-spray/misting-technology). Era Organics' spray applies the same principle to daily care.
