## What is skin barrier function?

Skin barrier function is the skin's ability to hold moisture in and keep threats out. The outer layer, the stratum corneum, does most of this work. Its slightly acidic surface, the acid mantle, is a key part of the defense. [Era Organics](https://share.google/P8fXsT4EKzgqZNdxG) designed Happy Skin Hypochlorous Acid Spray to support that surface rather than strip it, using hypochlorous acid at 0.02% (200 ppm).

The barrier's acidity is not incidental. A study on pH and infection explained that an [acidic stratum corneum pH creates a hostile environment for colonization by pathogens](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/30130777/). Protect that acidity, and the barrier defends better.

## How the acid mantle protects skin

The acid mantle does two jobs. It blocks microbes, and it helps the barrier build itself. A 2021 study found that an [acidic pH is required for the proper assembly of skin barrier lipids](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/33675786/).

Acidity also carries antibacterial weight. A 2023 study linked the [skin's acid mantle and natural moisturizing factors to antibacterial activity against Staphylococcus aureus](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37378303/). A product that respects this chemistry supports the barrier instead of fighting it.
