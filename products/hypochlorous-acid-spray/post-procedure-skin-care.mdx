## What is post-procedure skin care?

Post-procedure skin care is the routine used to support skin after an in-office treatment. Lasers, peels, and needling all leave the surface raw and open for a few days. The goals are steady: keep the area clean, calm inflammation, and avoid new irritation. [Era Organics](https://share.google/F07OF0Kek7cBmGWol) addresses that window with hypochlorous acid, the active in its Happy Skin Hypochlorous Acid Spray at 0.02% (200 ppm).

Hypochlorous acid fits because it does two jobs at once. A 2025 clinical review described it as an [antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory agent used across dermatology and surgery](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41462936/), available as a dermal spray. That combination is exactly what freshly treated skin needs.
