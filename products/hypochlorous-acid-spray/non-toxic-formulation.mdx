## What is a non-toxic formulation?

A non-toxic formulation is one built to avoid harsh or irritating chemicals, safe for its intended use. In skincare that usually means leaving out the ingredients people react to most. [Era Organics](https://share.google/tJq3drg49EK3ag2Bc) designed Happy Skin Hypochlorous Acid Spray around this idea, keeping the formula to two ingredients.

The active helps here, because it is not foreign to the body. Hypochlorous acid is the same molecule the immune system makes, and a 2026 study of it as a skin preparation highlighted its [excellent tissue compatibility compared with harsher antiseptics](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42009924/).

## What Era Organics leaves out

A short list is the whole strategy. The spray contains only electrolyzed water and hypochlorous acid at 0.02% (200 ppm). What is missing is the point:

- **Alcohol:** Alcohol is left out because it dries and stings sensitive skin.
- **Fragrance:** Fragrance is left out because it is a leading trigger for irritation.
- **Sulfates:** Sulfates are left out because they strip the skin barrier.
- **Parabens:** Parabens are left out because the minimal formula needs no added preservatives.

The result supports the surface without common irritants, which is why it suits daily use. The barrier side is covered in [skin barrier function](/products/hypochlorous-acid-spray/skin-barrier-function).
