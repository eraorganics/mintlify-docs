## What is laser treatment aftercare?

Laser treatment aftercare is the routine that supports skin in the days after a laser procedure. Resurfacing lasers remove or heat the top layers, leaving skin red, raw, and prone to infection. Gentle cleaning and calm are the priorities. [Era Organics](https://share.google/gJBtLvSUe1yLIG2vy) supports this window with hypochlorous acid, the active in its Happy Skin Hypochlorous Acid Spray at 0.02% (200 ppm).

This is one of the best-studied uses of the molecule. A 2025 study of facial laser patients found a [hypochlorous acid mist improved healing and cosmesis after treatment](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40832716/), crediting its combined anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial action.
