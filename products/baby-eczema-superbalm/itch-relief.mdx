## The evidence behind colloidal oatmeal

Colloidal oatmeal has real clinical support for calming itchy, eczema-prone skin. A double-blind study of an [over-the-counter 1% colloidal oatmeal cream](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/28366039/) in children found it effective and well tolerated for mild to moderate atopic dermatitis compared with a prescription barrier cream.

More recent work backs the everyday routine. A 2025 study of a [1% colloidal oatmeal cream and baby wash regimen](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41037526/) reported improvement in pediatric atopic dermatitis from first use. That is the same active that carries Superbalm's soothing of [pruritus](/conditions/itchy-skin), the clinical name for itch.
