## What are zinc oxide ointments?

Zinc oxide ointments are thick, white barrier pastes best known as classic diaper-rash creams. Zinc oxide forms a physical layer that shields irritated skin from moisture and friction while the skin recovers. A study using [electrometric assessment of a zinc oxide paste in diaper dermatitis](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/19467034/) measured its effect on the barrier and water-holding of diapered skin.

These pastes are effective for the diaper area, though their opaque, sticky texture is made for barrier duty rather than all-over daily moisturizing. That is where their role differs from a soothing balm.

## Barrier protection across the body

Managing diaper rash is fundamentally about protecting damaged skin from irritants, whichever product does the sealing. A [systematic review of diaper dermatitis care](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41014074/) analyzed studies in nearly three thousand children and reinforced barrier protection and moisture control as the core of prevention and treatment.
