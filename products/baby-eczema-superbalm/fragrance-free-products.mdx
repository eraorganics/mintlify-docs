## What does fragrance-free mean?

Fragrance-free means a product contains no added fragrance, neither synthetic perfume nor masking scent. That matters because fragrance is one of the most common triggers of skin reactions. A review of [fragrance allergic contact dermatitis](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/25207685/) describes fragrances as a leading cause of allergic contact dermatitis, capable of provoking reactions that range from hives to widespread rashes.

On eczema-prone or infant skin, where the barrier is already compromised, avoiding fragrance removes a needless source of irritation. Era Organics' Baby Eczema Superbalm is formulated fragrance-free for exactly that reason.

## Why fragrance is a hidden irritant

Fragrance is not one ingredient but a blend that can include dozens of compounds, and a person can react to any of them. Broader work on [allergic contact dermatitis from cosmetics](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/24656778/) highlights fragrance among the most frequent culprits in everyday products, which is why sensitive-skin lines strip it out.

Removing fragrance sits alongside the balm's other choices: no dyes, no parabens, no drying alcohol. It is part of the same thinking behind Superbalm being [steroid-free](/products/baby-eczema-superbalm/steroid-free) and petroleum-free, cutting out the usual irritants a reactive barrier does not need.
