## What is hydrocortisone?

Hydrocortisone is a low-potency topical corticosteroid, the most common over-the-counter steroid for calming eczema flares. It works by suppressing inflammation in the skin, which quickly reduces redness and itch. It is a useful short-term tool, and its low strength makes it the mildest of the prescription and non-prescription steroids.

Its trade-off is well documented. A comprehensive review of [topical corticosteroid-induced skin atrophy](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/25862024/) describes thinning of the skin as an established adverse effect of repeated steroid use, one reason these creams are meant for limited courses rather than indefinite daily application.

## Why families look past the steroid
