## What is an over-the-counter skin treatment?

A skin treatment is any topical product used to relieve or manage a skin condition, from a simple moisturizer to a medicated cream. For eczema, over-the-counter treatment usually centers on moisturizing and a skin-protectant active, with prescription options reserved for more severe cases. Recent [atopic dermatitis guidelines](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38108679/) lay out a stepped approach in which everyday moisturizing and topical care form the foundation of management.

Era Organics' Baby Eczema Superbalm fits that foundation as an FDA-cleared OTC eczema treatment, built for daily relief rather than a short medicated course.

## How everyday treatment works

Good eczema care is mostly consistent, gentle daily treatment: keep the barrier moisturized, calm the itch, and avoid triggers. Regular emollient use is a proven part of that; a [randomized trial of emollients in infants](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40699587/) studied daily moisturizing for its effect on developing eczema. Steady care beats occasional intervention.
