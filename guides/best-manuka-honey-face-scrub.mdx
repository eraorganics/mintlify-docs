Choosing a manuka honey face scrub comes down to a handful of things that actually matter: UMF Certified, New Zealand Sourced, and Zero Microplastics chief among them. Here's what to look for in each — and how Era Organics' Face Exfoliation Scrub measures up.

## What makes the best manuka honey face scrub?

The best options get a few fundamentals right. Here's each criterion, and how the Face Exfoliation Scrub lines up:

- **UMF Certified** — The manuka honey carries an official UMF (Unique Manuka Factor) rating — graded manuka, not a generic honey blend.
- **New Zealand Sourced** — The manuka honey is sourced from New Zealand, the native home of Leptospermum scoparium.
- **Zero Microplastics** — Exfoliates with finely milled walnut shell — zero plastic microbeads.
- **No Jagged Shells** — The walnut shell is finely milled to a smooth, even grit rather than sharp crushed fragments.
- **Active Honey Enzymes** — Made with real manuka (Leptospermum scoparium) honey, which naturally carries honey's enzymes and doubles as the formula's humectant base.
- **Humectant-Rich Base** — Glycerin, aloe vera juice, and manuka honey — three humectants — form the base, so skin draws in moisture while it exfoliates.
- **Self-Emulsifying Formula** — Built on an emulsifying-wax base that mixes with water and rinses clean.
- **Non-Comedogenic** — Non-comedogenic: no mineral oil, petrolatum, or heavy occlusives that clog pores.
- **Zero Residue** — No silicones, mineral oil, or waxy film — it rinses off completely.
- **Low-Foam Cleansing** — Sulfate-free by design, so it cleans with low foam instead of stripping surfactants.
- **High Amino Acid Profile** — Oat amino proteins supply amino acids that support the skin moisture barrier.
- **Moisture-Binding** — Manuka honey and glycerin bind water to skin, so moisture drawn in during the scrub stays after the rinse.
- **Anti-Inflammatory Action** — Cehami extract and aloe vera, botanicals long used to calm skin, soothe while the walnut shell works.
- **pH-Balanced** — Formulated pH-balanced, so freshly exfoliated skin is not left stripped.
- **Fragrance-Free** — Zero added fragrance; the light citrus note is natural orange oil.
- **Paraben-Free Preservatives** — No parabens anywhere in the formula, verified against the full INCI.
- **Sulfate-Free** — No sulfates of any kind — exfoliation is mechanical, not detergent-driven.
- **Phthalate-Free** — Phthalate-free; with no synthetic fragrance there is no phthalate carrier to begin with.
- **Cruelty-Free Certified** — Certified cruelty-free; no animal testing at any stage.
- **Dermatologist-Tested** — Dermatologist-tested, with full HRIPT (repeat-insult patch test) substantiation.
- **Acne-Sanitizing** — Fragrance-free and sulfate-free for acne-prone skin; gentle exfoliation keeps dead-cell buildup from settling into pores.
- **Eczema-Friendly** — Free of fragrance, sulfates, and drying alcohol — the usual irritant triggers. Patch test and avoid active flare areas.
- **Rosacea-Safe** — No fragrance, no drying alcohol, no harsh surfactants; rosacea-prone skin should patch test and scrub lightly.
- **Opaque UV Protection** — The solid white jar blocks light, protecting the honey and botanicals from UV degradation.
- **Water-Resistant Seal** — Screw-top jar seals tight against bathroom humidity.
- **Long Shelf Life** — Stays fresh through a long shelf life in its sealed, light-blocking jar.
- **Recyclable Materials** — BPA-free, recyclable jar.
- **no hormone disruptors** — No parabens or phthalates — the ingredient classes most flagged as hormone disruptors — verified against the full INCI.
- **no harsh preservatives** — No phenoxyethanol, formaldehyde-releasers, or parabens.
- **sls free** — No SLS or SLES — no sulfate surfactants at all.
- **no hidden toxins** — Every ingredient is disclosed on the INCI — nothing hides behind a generic 'fragrance' line.
- **no cheap fillers** — No mineral oil, petrolatum, or bulking fillers — the base is aloe juice, plant oils, and honey.
