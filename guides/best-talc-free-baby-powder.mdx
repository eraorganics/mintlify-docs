Choosing a talc-free baby powder comes down to a handful of things that actually matter: No Hormone Disruptors, No Harsh Preservatives, and Sulfate Free chief among them. Here's what to look for in each — and how Era Organics' Talc Free Baby Powder measures up.

## What makes the best talc-free baby powder?

The best options get a few fundamentals right. Here's each criterion, and how the Talc Free Baby Powder lines up:

- **No Hormone Disruptors** — Free of the usual endocrine-disruptor suspects — no phthalates, no parabens, and no synthetic fragrance anywhere in the six-ingredient formula.
- **No Harsh Preservatives** — A dry, water-free powder that needs no chemical preservative system — no parabens, phenoxyethanol, or formaldehyde-releasers; rosemary extract and mixed tocopherols keep the botanicals fresh naturally.
- **Sulfate Free** — Contains no sulfates of any kind — just arrowroot powder, cornstarch, kaolin clay, calendula, rosemary extract, and tocopherols.
- **SLS Free** — No sodium lauryl sulfate or any other sulfate surfactant in the formula.
- **No Hidden Toxins** — The complete ingredient list is six items you can read at a glance — no talc, no formaldehyde, no synthetic dyes, nothing undisclosed.
- **No Cheap Fillers** — Every ingredient earns its place: arrowroot, cornstarch, and kaolin clay do the moisture-absorbing work, while calendula and rosemary extract soothe — no talc or inert bulking agents.
- **talcum free** — Zero talc — moisture absorption comes from plant-derived arrowroot powder, cornstarch, and natural kaolin clay instead.
- **dye free** — No synthetic dyes or added colorants — the powder is the natural color of its botanical ingredients.
- **fragrance free** — Completely fragrance-free — no added fragrance, a fit for scent-sensitive skin from babies to adults.
- **perfume free** — No perfume or masking scent compounds of any kind — nothing is added to make it smell like anything.
