## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do you brighten dull skin and get a glow?

Dull skin usually comes down to a buildup of dead cells sitting on the surface. That layer flattens your skin tone and texture, so light doesn't reflect evenly and the complexion reads tired instead of glowing. The most direct way to bring back radiance is to lift that layer away with gentle physical exfoliation.

This is exactly what the Revive+ Microdermabrasion Manuka Honey Walnut Scrub & Mask is built around. Finely milled walnut shell powder (Juglans Regia Shell Powder) physically sweeps away the dead cells dulling the surface, buffing away the rough, uneven layer rather than letting it accumulate. Once that layer is gone, orange oil (Citrus Sinensis Oil) brightens the freshly revealed surface, leaving the complexion looking visibly clearer.

Think of it as a two-step logic in one product: clear the old layer first, then support brightness on the new one. Fresh skin cells simply reflect light better than a crust of dead ones, which is where that healthy-looking glow comes from.

A practical rhythm: exfoliate a couple of times a week rather than daily, be gentle with pressure, and patch test any new scrub first. Consistency, not intensity, is what keeps dullness from creeping back.

### How do you get glowing skin naturally?

Getting glowing skin naturally usually comes down to removing what's hiding it, not layering on more product. Dead skin cells settle into a film across the surface, and that layer flattens both your tone and your texture — it's a big reason a complexion reads dull instead of radiant. Buff that layer away and the fresher skin underneath can actually show through.

That's why physical exfoliation is the classic natural route to a glow. Walnut shell powder (Juglans Regia Shell Powder) physically sweeps away the layer of dead cells that dulls the surface, and orange oil (Citrus Sinensis Oil) then brightens the newly revealed skin, leaving the complexion visibly clearer. It's a simple one-two: clear the buildup first, then support a brighter surface.

This pairing is the idea behind Era Organics' Revive+ Microdermabrasion Manuka Honey Walnut Scrub & Mask — the walnut shell powder does the sweeping while the orange oil supports that visibly clearer, brighter look afterward. You don't need a complicated routine to look more radiant; softening the look of dullness starts with gently buffing away the dead-cell layer so your skin's natural glow isn't sitting under a flat, tired-looking surface.
