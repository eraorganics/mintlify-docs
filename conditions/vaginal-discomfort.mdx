### How do I soothe vaginal burning?

Burning-type vaginal discomfort is often tied to dryness — especially around perimenopause and menopause, when declining estrogen thins and dries vulvovaginal tissue. Keeping that delicate skin moisturized externally can help soothe and calm the irritation. ### What causes vaginal burning, pain, or discomfort?

One common cause of vaginal burning, pain, or discomfort — especially during perimenopause and menopause — is declining estrogen, which thins and dries the vulvovaginal tissue. That thinning, drier tissue is more easily irritated, which can show up as burning or general discomfort.
