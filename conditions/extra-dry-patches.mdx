Shea butter (Butyrospermum Parkii) delivers dense fatty acids that coat and soften keratinized skin. Sodium hyaluronate draws moisture into the outer skin layer (stratum corneum), holding it there through repeated friction and movement. Extra-dry patches on the vulva soften with daily application of a dime-sized amount.
