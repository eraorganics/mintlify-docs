## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do you treat, soothe, and prevent bug bites?

Bug bites and minor skin irritations share a common need: a clean, protective environment that keeps the skin surface stable while irritation settles. The practical step after a bite is covering and calming the affected area without introducing ingredients that compound the problem.

Era Organics Superbalm works here through its occlusive base of Beeswax and Coconut Oil, which forms a protective layer over the skin. This keeps the bitten area covered and moisturized without synthetic fragrances or dyes — additives that can aggravate sensitive skin and worsen discomfort.

For anyone whose skin reacts easily, avoiding those irritants makes a real difference. A formula free from synthetic fragrances and dyes gives skin room to calm without additional triggers. Superbalm's protective, occlusive base makes it a practical choice for comforting and soothing minor bug-bite irritation, particularly on skin that is already sensitive or reactive.
