### How do you soothe chafing on the skin?

Chafing happens when repeated friction wears down the uppermost skin layer, leaving high-contact areas like the inner thighs and underarms red and raw. The goal with soothing it is to reduce ongoing friction and protect the irritated skin surface.

Era Organics Superbalm is a practical option for this. Arrowroot Powder in the formula absorbs surface moisture, while Beeswax reduces friction directly at the skin surface. Together they create a protective film that stays in place during movement — which means it keeps working through the activity that triggered the chafing, not just when you're at rest.
